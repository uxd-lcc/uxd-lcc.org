Your Assignment

This unit offers the chance to engage in an additional selected design competition project.

You will be presented with a range of briefs or open calls, selected from design competitions, award schemes and international art and design festivals. You will choose an appropriate brief based on your interests and current or desired design practice. You will analyse the brief and requirements, identify audience,project scopeand develop an appropriate proposal and design response. You may also find your own briefs or calls to respond to in discussion with your tutors.

The response to the brief will take the form of a proposal aimed at the awarding body or judges of the brief, reflecting this in tone and content. This document should focus on research findings/insights, evidence for your design decisions and documenting any proof of concept or prototype you make. You should carefully consider how to present your research -focussing on how to make your thinking visible in a well-designed and professional way.

The PDF submission will include a link to this proposal and a detailed reflection on and documentation of your learning, which will demonstrate that you have met the learning outcomes.

Learning Outcomes

The unit learning outcomes and how they can be evidenced
On completion of this unit you will be able to: You will show evidence of this by:
Produce innovative and appropriate solutions to high professional standards. (Process, Realisation, Knowledge) Your response to the brief will show your abilities to develop and implement design solutions appropriate to the chosen brief, depending on the requirements of the stakeholders
Define the essential design task to be executed, the intended audience(s) and prioritise the elements to answer a design brief. (Enquiry) You will demonstrate how you have synthesised your research to produce an appropriate design, and how you have incorporated the needs of your projected audience.
Identify and solve design problems in innovative, appropriate and sustainable ways that leverage your specialist skill set and present these findings at a professional level. (Communication) You will provide evidence of your personal abilities and how you position them relative to the context of the brief, you will demonstrate how you present your work to a wider professional audience.
